Schools

Roberts Elementary Closed Due to Norovirus Outbreak

Over 130 students and staff affected; deep cleaning underway

Roberts Elementary School remains closed following a suspected norovirus outbreak that has affected more than 130 students and staff members. The school has undergone deep cleaning and officials are monitoring the situation closely to determine when it will be safe to reopen.

Parents are being kept updated on the status, and the district is working to minimize disruption to the academic calendar.

Why it matters: School closures impact families who must arrange childcare and disrupt educational continuity across the district during a critical time in the academic year.
